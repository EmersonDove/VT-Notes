# Integration identities

$$
\\Polar\\
r^2=x^2+y^2\\
x=r\cos(\theta)\\
y=r\sin(\theta)\\[30pt]

\\Cartesian\ to\ Cylindrical\\
\\Notice\ this\ is\ Polar\ with\ z=z\\
x=r \cos(\theta)\\
y=r \sin(\theta)\\
z=z\\[10pt]

\\Spherical\ to\ Cylindrical\\
r=\rho \sin(\phi)\\
\theta = \theta\\
z = \rho \cos(\phi)\\[10pt]

\rho^2 = r^2+z^2\\[30pt]

\\Spherical\\
\rho^2=x^2+y^2+z^2\\
\\Notice\ this\ is\ polar\ with\ z\ defined\\
x=r \cos(\theta)\\
y=r \sin(\theta)\\
z=\rho \cos(\phi)\\[10pt]
x=\rho \sin(\phi) \cos(\theta)\\
y=\rho \sin(\phi) \sin(\theta)\\
z=\rho \cos(\phi)\\
$$
